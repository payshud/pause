Paysen Hudson

Hi, my name is Paysen Hudson! I am 17 years old, a senior in high school, and a sophomore in college. I am getting an associates degree in computer science and I hope to go into a technology career! My partners and I decided to make this website because we felt as if there were little resources for teenagers to help them with their mental health. Any app or website seems to require money and we want to allow teenagers a way to only need to invest time instead of money. We believe mental health is important and there should be an easily accessible way to get information. We are not medical professionals, so this website is a collection of professional resources to give it’s users an easy path to learning their mind!
